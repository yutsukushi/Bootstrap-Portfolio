About Me

Hi there! As you may have found out, my name is Yukie. I am Front-end developer. My interest in web development first stemmed from playing old flash games on Yahoo: Yahooligans. It was my first exposure to the World Wide Web and haven't looked back since!

Described with having undying determination for self-development and meticulousness for design towards responsive web design. I am enthusiastic to be a part of a goal-driven, communicative team environment to strive for quality user interfaces and functionality.

I have a creative background in 3D modelling, texture design, and rendering, as well as, fine art. I played a visual role for a team in creating an educational game for children in Bosnia to interning for VR/AR start-up companies.


Outside of my professional development, I am an aquarium hobbyist, avian enthusiast, animal shelter volunteer and have a definite sweet spot for video games and movies! It's safe to say I am keen on animal welfare. I am also very invested into being environmentally conscious.
