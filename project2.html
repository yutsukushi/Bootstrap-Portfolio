All-you-can-find contemporary art hub!


Our app lets you search and save some of your favorite art based on a search for an artist’s name. Users can create a user account and save them to their profile to access them later. Users can also delete them if they no longer want to keep them. Just create a profile to get started!


My Responsibilities


  • UI Rendering
  • MVC structuring
  • Database Troubleshooting

UI Rendering


I built our application’s components to dynamically render the DOM every time a user searches an artist on the search page or favoriting an artwork to their profile page. I also worked on button functionality and page change rendering.


MVC Structuring


In charge of maintaining an organized folder structure, I separated back-end, front-end development and many subfolders of related files for ease of navigation and clarity.


Database Troubleshooting


My role in the back-end was not as prominent as the front-end development, however, I lent a hand in setting up and troubleshooting our database with MongoDB.
