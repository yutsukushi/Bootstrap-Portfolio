About Me

Hi there! As you may have found out, my name is Yukie. I am an aspiring User-Interface designer/developer. My interest in user-interface design/development first stemmed from playing old flash games on Yahoo: Yahooligans. It was my first exposure to the World Wide Web and haven't looked back since!

I have a creative background in 3D modelling, texture design, and rendering, as well as, contemporary paintings. I played a visual role for a team in creating an educational game for children in Bosnia to interning for VR/AR start-up companies.

Outside of my professional development, I am an aquarium hobbyist, avian enthusiast, animal shelter volunteer and have a definite sweet spot for video games and movies! It's definitely safe to say my interests lay in animal welfare. I am also very invested into being environmentally conscious.

Having the flexibility to thrive in teamwork environments and working solo, I enjoy the process of creation and look forward to working with you!
